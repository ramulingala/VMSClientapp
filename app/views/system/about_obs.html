OBS is a enterprise level Recurring Billing & CRM system meant for automating the subscription billing & entitlement management for IPTV, VOD, OTT, PayTV, DTH, ISP & Cable operators.

It supports all the processes which are essentially required by the service operators from Customer Acquisition, Payments, Service Entitlements to Customer Retention Management.

Each module are tightly integrated to ensure the information is shared across the modules for accurate information and to attain Customer satisfaction. Below are the features of each module of OBS.

Inventory & Logistics Inventory & Logistics
  • Define the Items with warranty and price, Supplier
  • Buy the stock from supplier in to warehouse / office called as GRN
  • Modify the Item details like Quality / Provisioning Details
  • Item Sales to Agents Items Transfers to Offices
  • Raise a Complaint / Ticket
  • Edit the Ticket by user with additional Information
  • Close the Resolved Tickets
  • Create a New Prospect in the system
  • Edit the Prospect with the follow up details
  • Cancel the Prospect or Convert the prospect into a client
Client & Portfolio Billing & Finance
  • Creating / Modifying a New Client, Adding the additional information like Address, Identity, Documents, Credit Card Information and Note
  • Booking Pre-paid and Postpaid orders Disconnection, Reconnection, Renewal After and Before Expiry, Extension, CAS Commands. Pairing and Un Pairing
  • Charge codes are associated with tax Service and Plan, Region wise Pricing Invoicing with Tax and without Payment Information
  • Credit and Debit Adjustment
Provisioning & Integrations Administration & Reports
  • CAS: Comvenient, NSTV
  • ISP: 24Online, Packet Shaper, Netspan
  • Middleware : Stalker, Beenius,Payment Gateways,Paypal One Time and Recurring CCAvenue Authorize.net,Mpesa, Tigo Pesa
  • Bulk Data Uploads for Item Details, MRN, Payment, Client, Order, Credit and Debit Notes, Event & EPG
  • Scheduler Jobs for Invoicing, Statement, Provisioning, Messaging (Email / SMS / OSD Notifications).
