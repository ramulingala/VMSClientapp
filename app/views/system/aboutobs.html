@2013 Copyright Open Billing System OBS is a enterprise level Recurring Billing & CRM system meant for automating the subscription billing & entitlement management for IPTV, VOD, OTT, PayTV, DTH, ISP & Cable operators. It supports all the processes which are essentially required by the service operators from Customer Acquisition, Payments, Service Entitlements to Customer Retention Management. Each module are tightly integrated to ensure the information is shared across the modules for accurate information and to attain Customer satisfaction.
